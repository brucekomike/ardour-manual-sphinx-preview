# The Right Mouse
Ardour is designed to work best with a [three button mouse]{.dfn}
equipped with a [scroll wheel]{.dfn}. While it can be used with a two
button mouse or touchpad, at least two key operations will not be
(easily) available:

- time-constrained region copying
- [[MIDI]{.abbr title="Musical
    Instrument Digital Interface"} bindings](@@generic-midi-learn)
  created by \"learning\" them from incoming MIDI data

It is strongly encouraged to invest in a three-button mouse. A good
quality mouse (especially one with a weighted, latchable scroll wheel)
will make the use of Ardour vastly more efficient. They are cheap, and
time is not.

For information on how to use the mouse in Ardour, see [Using the
mouse](@@using-the-mouse).
